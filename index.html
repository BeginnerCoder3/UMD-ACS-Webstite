About the chapter

A community built around chemistry.

Fig.02 · DNA double helix All majors welcome

The ACS Student Chapter at the University of Maryland is open to undergraduate and graduate students enrolled at UMD who are interested in the field of chemistry. Our chapter is dedicated to promoting chemistry and its related fields through scientific excellence and rigor, professional mentorship, and meaningful community engagement.

We strive to provide members with career and educational guidance, cultivate networking opportunities within and outside our university, and develop a lasting commitment to service and outreach. The goal is to prepare you for your future career in STEM.

How to Join

Getting involved takes about three steps.

1

Register

Fill out the membership form to get on our roster and start getting updates about events.

2

Attend Events

Come to at least three events a semester. That can be a speaker event, community service, a lab tour, or a social gathering.

3

Give Back

Finish your service hours each semester through our outreach events and stay in good standing.

Eligibility

Membership Requirements

  • Be a current University of Maryland undergraduate or graduate student.
  • Be registered to take or have previously taken at least one CHEM or BCHM course at UMD.
  • Attend at least three events per semester and complete the required service hours.
  • Maintain at least a 2.0 GPA.
  • Be excited about STEM, networking, and community service.
  • National American Chemical Society membership is optional.
