01 / About The chapter

A student community for anyone into chemistry. We run events, help each other through the hard classes, tutor for free, and get out into the community around College Park.

Who

Every major welcome

Members

Undergrad and grad

Since

You do not need to be a chem major or have any lab experience. You just need to be curious. We will help you find research, point you toward opportunities, and introduce you to people worth knowing.

03 / Why join Three good reasons
01

Professional development

We run resume workshops, mock interviews, and talks with chemists from industry and the faculty. It is good practice for the things that actually come up when you apply to jobs and grad school.

02

Mentorship and networking

Get to know upperclassmen, alumni, and professors who have already done what you are trying to do. It is how a lot of our members find research advisors, study partners, and honestly just friends.

03

Outreach and service

We visit local schools, run demos, and take on service projects. You give back to the community and knock out your service hours at the same time.

06 / Join Takes two minutes

Join the chapter.

What we ask

  • 01Be a current UMD undergraduate or graduate student.
  • 02Be taking, or have taken, at least one CHEM or BCHM course at UMD.
  • 03Come to at least three events a semester and finish your service hours.
  • 04Keep at least a 2.0 GPA.
  • 05Be into STEM, meeting people, and giving back. National ACS membership is optional.
